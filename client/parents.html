    All data collected during the hackathon is for test purposes only and discarded. The architecture is such that Penguin servers don't retain content -- they simply orchestrate between users end clients and APIs for cognitive processing. Our site is not intended to collect any personal information from Under 13s rather just provide a closed community for safe communications.
